Fifteenth anniversary event

   On September 23 at 5:00 pm, the "Confucius Institute Day 2016" and the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Confucius Institute at Latvia University and the 25th anniversary celebration of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Latin America were held at the auditorium of the University of Latvia. The theme of this celebration is " Pull a family ".

   From the Embassy of China, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Latvia, Xinhua News Agency, University of Latvia, Confucius Institute at the University of Latvia, University of Riga, University of Daugava, University of Latvia, University of Communication and Telecommunications, Riga Cultural Secondary School, 34 Middle School in Riga, Vasipiladuo Middle School, Chinese Overseas Chinese Federation, Huawei Company, Latvian Students' Association of Latvia, Latvian Wushu Association, Lazhong Cultural Association, Northern Lights Cultural Center Charm Band, Future Art Studio, Hip-hop Institute and other institutions and the community More than 200 people attended the event.

   Prof. Shang Qiu Yu, the president of Confucius Institute, and Prof. Bedh Gao, president of Lafang University, first extended a warm welcome to all the guests present at the scene and addressed them in Chinese and Latvian respectively. The two deans mentioned that after five years of development, the scale of running Confucius Institutes has been continuously expanded, the number of teaching points and students has been on the rise, showing a thriving scene closely linked with the concern and support of people from all walks of life. The two presidents expressed their sincere gratitude to all the guests present at this meeting and hoped that Confucius Chinese teaching will continue to contribute greatly to the sound development of China-Latin America relations.

   Subsequently, Professor Mu Izaz, President of the University of Latvia, Counselor Li Tao of the Chinese Embassy, ??and Chairman Zhang Fang of the Asia-Europe Division of the Latvian Foreign Ministry delivered speeches respectively, expressing their congratulation on the 5th anniversary of the establishment of Confucius Institute and the 25th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Latin America. . In his speech, President Muizenxz fully affirmed the achievements of the Confucius Institute and encouraged all faculty and staff of Confucius Institute to make persistent efforts to continuously improve the quality of teaching and continue to expand the influence of Confucius Institute. Counselor Li Tao mentioned that as an important part of educational exchanges between China and Latin America, Confucius Institute is responsible for training Chinese talents and cultural exchange messengers. He hopes that the young people in both countries can make better use of the Confucius Institute as a platform to lay the foundation for the multi-level and wide-ranging exchanges and cooperation between the two countries. Zhang Fang pointed out that he hopes Confucius Institute can train more Latvian new generation of sinologists and serve for the country's economic development and the friendly exchange between the two countries.
